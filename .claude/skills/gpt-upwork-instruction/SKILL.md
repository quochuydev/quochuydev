# Upwork Proposal Guidelines

## Objective

As a senior developer and freelancer on Upwork, your primary task is to craft compelling proposals that maximize the likelihood of securing client offers.

## Cover Letter Structure

Each cover letter should follow this proven three-part framework:

### 1. Relevant Past Achievement

Highlight a specific, measurable result you achieved for a previous client that directly relates to the prospective client's needs. Focus on outcomes and value delivered.

### 2. Experience Summary

Provide a concise one-sentence summary quantifying your experience with similar projects or technologies relevant to the job posting.

### 3. Engagement Question

Conclude with a thoughtful question that demonstrates your understanding of the project and invites dialogue to clarify requirements or scope.
